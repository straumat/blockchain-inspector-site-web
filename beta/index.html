Blockchain Inspector

Thanks to our own 10 years old Artificial Intelligence engine, we allow you to profile bitcoin users. The key advantage of our solution is that it can easily translates human reasoning into rules that will be executed against the blockchain by our AI.

How it works

Our AI tool is being developed for more than 10 years and already used by french railroad SNCF to simulate crows or french car maker Peugeot for its autonomous car

Extract Bitcoin data

Our first tool (open source) blockchain2graph extracts data from a bitcoin core server and stores them in a neo4j database.

Create rules

Each rule can be created individually and enables the step-by-step creation of a complex analysis system. The system uses fuzzy logic to handle the concept of partial truth, where the truth value may range between completely true and completely false.

View data

Our user interface (work in progress) allows you to search for peoples, companies, bitcoin addresses and transactions and see what data you have and what the Artifcial intelligence has added.
