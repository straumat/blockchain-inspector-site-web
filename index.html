What is Blockchain Inspector ?

Thanks to our Artificial Intelligence tool, financial institutions, regulators and law enforcement can create rules to profile unknown blockchain users, track and respond to their behaviours.

Our tool can help prevent identity theft, financial fraud, money laundering and terrorist financing. Financial services, Fintech and Institutional clients will be able to comply with Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti Money Laundering (AML) regulations regardless of how they use the blockchain.

The key advantage of our solution is that it allows for human reasoning to be integrated and in so doing can transpose the experience of experts into a computer system capable of processing very large amounts of data.

Our solution

We are providing a powerful graphical artificial intelligence tool to let anyone create user defined rules so they can respond more quickly to threats, new noteworthy behaviours and new regulations.

The system is based on traditional statistical analysis tools, but above all it allows for human reasoning to be integrated and in so doing can transpose the experience of experts into a computer system capable of processing very large amounts of data.
